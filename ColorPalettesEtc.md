upload an image here: https://www.canva.com/colors/color-palette-generator/

## or do it in R
  
```R
library(devtools)
install_github("andreacirilloac/paletter")
library(paletter)

x = create_palette(image_path = "yourimage.jpg",
               number_of_colors = 32,
               type_of_variable = "categorical")
  ```
